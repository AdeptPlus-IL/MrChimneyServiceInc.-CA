Common Questions

Chimney Sweeping FAQs

Get answers to common questions about our chimney sweeping and maintenance services.

How often should I get my chimney swept?

It's recommended to have your chimney swept at least once a year, ideally before the start of the heating season. If you burn wood frequently, you may need it cleaned more often to prevent dangerous creosote buildup.

How long does a chimney sweeping service take?

A standard chimney sweeping typically takes between 45 minutes to 1 hour. More complex jobs involving heavy creosote removal or inspections may take up to 90 minutes.

What are the signs that my chimney needs cleaning?

Common signs include a strong odor coming from the fireplace, black soot falling into the firebox, poor drafting or smoke entering the room, and visible creosote buildup inside the flue. If you notice any of these, schedule a cleaning right away.
