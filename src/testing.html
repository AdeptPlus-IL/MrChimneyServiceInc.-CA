Our Brick Repointing Process

Professional workmanship from start to finish

Our comprehensive brick repointing process ensures your chimney receives expert care at every stage, protecting your investment for years to come.

Professional Inspection

We thoroughly assess your chimney's mortar condition to identify repointing needs and any underlying structural issues.

Damage Assessment

Our CSIA certified team evaluates the extent of deterioration and discusses findings transparently with you.

Preparation

We carefully remove deteriorated mortar using specialized tools and techniques that protect the surrounding brick.

Repointing Work

Expert technicians apply new mortar using period-appropriate techniques and materials matched to your original chimney.

Complete quality check ensures your chimney is safe, functional, and looking great for years to come.
